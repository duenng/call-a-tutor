Playgroup

We make it possible for you to bring the whole playgroup settings to your home. All wrapped up in an extremely fun and perfectly safe package!

Our playgroup program is especially designed to provide children with a stimulating, productive environment, which is focused on strengthening their social skills.

Our playgroup serves children from 2 years to 5 years old. The group is small sized and conducted by experienced teacher.

We can provide playgroup teacher qualified to the following levels:

。Native speakers of Mandarin or Cantonese

。Putonghua Proficiency Test by the State Language Commission of China

。CPR and child first aid training

。Experience in tutoring toddlers and organizing playgroups
