Mandarin / Cantonese Speaking Nanny

Mandarin / Cantonese Speaking Nanny

Our philosophy is that Learning must be fun, practical and productive.

Children are so curious and eager to know everything and this friendly and playful setting makes their first contact with this second language completely natural.

Our team of nannies is well trained to take care of kids while providing a comforting Mandarin / Cantonese environment.

All of our nannies have immense practical experience to ensure their time is not only productive but very fun too.

We can provide playgroup teachers qualified to the following levels:

。Native speakers of Mandarin or Cantonese

。Putonghua Proficiency Test by the State Language Commission of China

。CPR and child first aid training

。Child care training
