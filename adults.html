Adults

For General Mandarin / Cantonese

We provide immersive and fun courses in daily conversational Chinese, including practice for real life situations, for example, ordering food, asking for directions, etc.

We also offer organized courses, teaching grammar and daily vocabulary, depending on the needs of the learner.

Also, while learning the Chinese characters isn't completely necessary, we do have courses where you can learn to write.

For Business Mandarin / Cantonese

For those who have attained a certain level of basic conversational skills and want to continue making progress, we offer business Chinese languages.

With our experienced tutors, you will receive tailor made lessons according to your industry needs.

We also provide corporate learning to help your employees understand Chinese.
