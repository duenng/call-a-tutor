Our mission is to provide the most engaging, effective and practical Mandarin / Cantonese and cultural training to anybody in need.

We provide the following programs to meet your needs:

Chinese Nannies - for Pre-school and Kindergarten Students

The program is especially designed to provide children with a stimulating, productive environment, which is focused on strengthening their social skills.

The early exposure to Chinese can help young kids to learn to speak in Chinese much more easily.

Tutoring for Primary and Secondary students

We help each student to attain their full potential, and help to improve confidence and grades.

Exams Preparation

In the process of learning, students might need to participate in different public exams to assess their levels. One-to-one tutoring is the best way to help make great progress. Our tutors can help students to prepare for the HSK, GAPSK, IGCSE, IB...
