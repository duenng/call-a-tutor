Our mission is to provide the most engaging, effective and practical Mandarin and cultural training to anybody in need (toddlers to adults).

。complete their academic studies successfully.

。communicate effectively with Mandarin speakers in practical themes.

We can provide tutors qualified to the following levels:

。Professionally trained to Bachelor / Master degree level

。Mandarin teaching training course (in the mainland China or Hong Kong)

。Teaching experience with good knowledge in the school curriculum
